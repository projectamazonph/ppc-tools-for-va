## Level 1: General VA → PPC Junior

**Timeline:** 6-12 months
**Salary Range:** ₱25,000 - ₱40,000/month

### What You Learn
- Amazon Seller Central navigation
- Basic product listing optimization
- Amazon Advertising Console basics
- Sponsored Products fundamentals
- Keyword research basics (Helium 10, Jungle Scout)
- Search term report reading
- Basic bid adjustments
- Daily account monitoring

### Skills to Develop
| Skill | How to Learn | Time |
|-------|-------------|------|
| Amazon Console navigation | Free Amazon Seller University | 1 week |
| Sponsored Products basics | Helium 10 free training | 2 weeks |
| Keyword research | Cerebro/Magnet tutorials | 2 weeks |
| Excel basics | YouTube tutorials | 2 weeks |
| Search term reports | Amazon Ads documentation | 1 week |

### Your First Tasks (as PPC Junior)
1. Download and organize search term reports
2. Flag keywords with 15+ clicks and 0 orders
3. Monitor daily budget pacing
4. Track campaign performance in spreadsheet
5. Assist senior specialist with bid adjustments

### How to Get Hired
- Complete Amazon Ads certification (free)
- Complete Helium 10 PPC course
- Build a portfolio with 2-3 practice accounts
- Apply to VA agencies (Project Amazon PH, OnlineJobs.ph)
- Start with $100-500/month accounts to build experience

## Level 2: PPC Junior → PPC Specialist

**Timeline:** 12-18 months
**Salary Range:** ₱40,000 - ₱65,000/month

### What You Learn
- Full campaign management (SP, SB, SD)
- Advanced keyword research and harvesting
- Bid optimization strategies
- Negative keyword management
- Budget allocation (70/20/10 rule)
- ACoS/ROAS optimization
- Client reporting and communication
- Campaign restructuring
- A/B testing

### Skills to Develop
| Skill | How to Learn | Time |
|-------|-------------|------|
| Sponsored Brands | Amazon Ads Academy | 2 weeks |
| Sponsored Display | Amazon Ads documentation | 1 week |
| Advanced bid optimization | Practice + case studies | 3 months |
| Client communication | Real client work | Ongoing |
| Campaign restructuring | Case studies + practice | 2 months |
| Helium 10 Adtomic | Tool documentation | 1 week |

### Your Responsibilities
1. Manage 3-5 accounts independently
2. Run weekly search term analysis
3. Optimize bids 2-3x per week
4. Create and manage campaign structures
5. Generate weekly client reports
6. Communicate with clients directly
7. Handle account launches

### How to Advance
- Manage accounts with $5K-20K monthly ad spend
- Document your results (ACoS improvements, sales growth)
- Build case studies from your work
- Get client testimonials
- Mentor junior VAs

## Portfolio Building Strategy

### Phase 1: Practice Accounts (Month 1-3)
- Create your own Amazon account (sell a small product)
- Run $5-10/day campaigns for 30 days
- Document everything: screenshots, metrics, changes
- Create before/after case study

### Phase 2: Pro Bono Work (Month 3-6)
- Offer free PPC management to 2-3 friends/family with Amazon stores
- Manage for 3 months minimum
- Document results with permission
- Get testimonials

### Phase 3: First Paid Client (Month 6+)
- Start with small accounts ($500-1000/month spend)
- Charge below market rate initially (₱15-20k/month)
- Over-deliver on reporting and communication
- Build case study from results

### Portfolio Pieces to Include
1. **Case Study 1:** Practice account results (before/after)
2. **Case Study 2:** Pro bono client results
3. **Case Study 3:** First paid client results
4. **Certification badges**
5. **Tool proficiency evidence** (Helium 10 screenshots)
6. **Client testimonials** (2-3 quotes)
7. **Sample reports** (anonymized)

## Salary Negotiation Tips

1. **Know your market rate:** Check OnlineJobs.ph, Upwork, LinkedIn
2. **Negotiate based on results:** "I improved ACoS by 30% — that's $X saved"
3. **Consider total compensation:** Salary + tools + training + flexibility
4. **Get it in writing:** Always have a contract
5. **Review every 6 months:** Ask for raise based on performance

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Changing too much too fast** — Let data accumulate before optimizing
2. **Ignoring search term reports** — This is where the gold is
3. **Not communicating with clients** — Silence creates anxiety
4. **Focusing only on ACoS** — Think TACoS and total business impact
5. **Not documenting changes** — When something works, you need to know why
6. **Automating without understanding** — Know the why before the how
7. **Spreading too thin** — Better to dominate 3 products than weakly support 10
