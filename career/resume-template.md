## Quantified Achievement Examples

### Instead of: "Managed PPC campaigns"
**Write:** "Managed 15 Amazon PPC campaigns across 8 products with $25K monthly ad spend"

### Instead of: "Improved ACoS"
**Write:** "Reduced ACoS from 45% to 22% within 60 days through keyword harvesting and bid optimization"

### Instead of: "Increased sales"
**Write:** "Drove 35% sales increase ($15K → $20K/month) while maintaining 25% ACoS"

### Instead of: "Did keyword research"
**Write:** "Conducted keyword research identifying 200+ opportunities, harvesting 45 high-converting terms to manual campaigns"

### Instead of: "Created reports"
**Write:** "Generated weekly performance reports for 5 clients, presenting data-driven recommendations that led to 3 account expansions"

### Instead of: "Reduced waste"
**Write:** "Negated 150+ underperforming keywords, reducing wasted ad spend by $3,200/month (28% savings)"

## Metrics to Include

| Metric | How to Find It | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| ACoS reduction | Before/after comparison | Optimization skill |
| ROAS improvement | Before/after comparison | Revenue impact |
| Revenue growth | Total sales change | Business impact |
| CPA reduction | Cost per acquisition change | Efficiency |
| CTR improvement | Click-through rate change | Ad relevance |
| CVR improvement | Conversion rate change | Listing quality |
| Budget managed | Total monthly ad spend | Scale |
| Accounts managed | Number of accounts | Experience breadth |
| Keywords optimized | Count of changes | Work ethic |
| Client retention | How long clients stay | Relationship skill |

## ATS Optimization Tips

### Keywords to Include (match the job description)
- Amazon PPC
- Sponsored Products
- Sponsored Brands
- Sponsored Display
- ACoS / ROAS
- Keyword Research
- Bid Optimization
- Search Term Analysis
- Campaign Management
- Helium 10
- Amazon Advertising Console
- Budget Management
- Client Reporting

### Formatting for ATS
- Use standard section headings
- No tables or columns (ATS can't read them)
- No images or graphics
- Save as PDF (preserves formatting)
- Use standard fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
