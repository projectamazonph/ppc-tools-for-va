## Phase 2: Calculator Tools (Week 1-2)

> Excel/Google Sheets calculators VAs use daily. High utility = high shares.

### 2.1 ACoS/ROAS Calculator
**File:** `calculators/acos-ros-calculator.xlsx`
- Input: Ad Spend, Ad Revenue
- Auto-calculate: ACoS, ROAS, Break-even ACoS, Profit Margin
- Scenario tab: "What if I increase spend by 20%?"
- Visual gauge: Green/Yellow/Red ACoS indicator

### 2.2 Bid Calculator
**File:** `calculators/bid-calculator.xlsx`
- Input: Target ACoS, Product Price, Conversion Rate, CPC
- Auto-calculate: Maximum CPC, Recommended Bid, Placement Adjustments
- Tab per match type: Exact, Phrase, Broad
- Scenario: "If CVR improves from 8% to 12%, what bid can I afford?"

### 2.3 Profit Margin Calculator
**File:** `calculators/profit-margin-calculator.xlsx`
- Input: Selling Price, COGS, FBA Fees, Shipping, PPC Spend
- Auto-calculate: Net Profit, Margin %, Break-even ACoS, Max PPC Spend
- Tab for multi-variant products (size/color variations)

### 2.4 Budget Planner
**File:** `calculators/budget-planner.xlsx`
- Input: Monthly Revenue Target, Target ACoS, Number of Products
- Auto-calculate: Monthly PPC Budget, Daily Budget per Product, Allocation by Priority
- 70/20/10 rule auto-applied
- Scenario: "What if I add 5 more products?"

### 2.5 Keyword ROI Calculator
**File:** `calculators/keyword-roi.xlsx`
- Input: Keyword data (impressions, clicks, orders, spend, revenue)
- Auto-calculate: CVR, ACoS, ROAS, Revenue per Click, Profit per Click
- Sort by: Highest ROI, Lowest ACoS, Most Volume
- Export: Harvest list, Negate list
