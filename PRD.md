# PRD — PPC Tools for Virtual Assistants

**Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** Approved | **Last Updated:** 2026-07-02

---

## Product Overview

PPC Tools for Virtual Assistants is a **free, open-source Amazon PPC resource library** purpose-built for Filipino VAs transitioning into PPC specialist roles. It contains quizzes, interview questions, case studies, SOPs, workflows, templates, automation guides, and career resources — all focused on real-world Amazon PPC skills.

**Core Value Proposition:** The complete PPC specialist toolkit — free, practical, and built by someone who's actually managed ₱50M+ in ad spend.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: VA Learners
- Filipino VAs with 0–2 years of PPC experience
- Self-directed learners wanting structured PPC education
- Job seekers preparing for PPC specialist interviews

### Secondary: Coaches & Agencies
- PPC coaches training VA students
- Agencies onboarding new PPC specialists
- Team leads standardizing PPC processes
